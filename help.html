To find the battery capacity and daily usage time on your iPhone, follow these simple steps:

1. Go to "Settings"
2. Tap on "Battery"
3. Under "Battery Health," you can find the maximum capacity of your battery in percentage.
4. To estimate your average daily usage time, go to "Settings," tap on "Battery," and look for "Screen Time." Here you will find the average time you spend using your phone per day.
